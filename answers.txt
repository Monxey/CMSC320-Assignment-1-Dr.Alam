Q3: What is the difference between merging and rebasing?

Answer: Merging pushes together the main branch with another branches commit. Essentially, the child branches contents are copied into the main branch.
This can lead to file conflicts sometimes. Rebasing replays the edits made in the child branch and saves those changes in the main branch.
